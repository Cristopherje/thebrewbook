The Classic Cappuccino

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of Whole Milk
  • Cocoa powder (optional)
  • Sugar to taste
  • 1 shot (1 oz) of Espresso

Steps

  1. Brew a shot of espresso into a cappuccino cup.
  2. Steam the milk until you have a thick layer of microfoam. The volume of the milk should double.
  3. Pour the steamed milk into the cup, aiming for the center. The foam should settle on top.
  4. Dust with cocoa powder if desired.
