Creamy Mocha

Description

A mocha is the perfect marriage between coffee and hot chocolate. It combines the intensity of espresso with sweet chocolate syrup and steamed milk, often topped with whipped cream. It is a dessert-like treat for coffee lovers.

Ingredients

  • 1 shot of Espresso
  • 1/2 cup of Steamed Milk
  • 2 tablespoons Chocolate Syrup
  • Whipped Cream
  • Shaved chocolate (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Brew a shot of espresso into a mug.
  2. Add the chocolate syrup to the hot espresso and stir well until dissolved.
  3. Steam the milk and pour it over the mixture.
  4. Top generously with whipped cream and sprinkle shaved chocolate on top.
