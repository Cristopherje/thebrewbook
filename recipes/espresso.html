The Perfect Espresso Shot

Ingredients

  • 18-20 grams of finely ground coffee beans
  • Hot water (filtered)

Steps

  1. Grind your coffee beans to a very fine texture, similar to table salt.
  2. Place the grounds into the portafilter and tamp (press) them down evenly with about 30 lbs of pressure.
  3. Lock the portafilter into the machine and start brewing immediately.
  4. Extract for about 25 to 30 seconds. You should end up with about 1 ounce of liquid with a golden crema on top. Serve immediately.
